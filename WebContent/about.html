1. What is Pantau?

Pantau is simply put, an Open Data Governance Platform. It helps instill transparency by making government projects available in the public domain so everyone can actively participate in the discourse.

3. Why Open Data Governance?

Pantau solves corruption from a different angle. We believe the root of corruption lies in the lack of documentation to justify legitimate financial flows.

We often hear that trillions of national budget is spent on dubious projects. Yet there is no way to clarify and question the legitimacy of such spendings simply because of the lack of information.

With Pantau, Indonesians can know what their government is doing right to the detail to establish accountability.

Transparency isn't just about access, it is also about sharing and reuse.

This is why we believe Open Data Governance will be crucial in building integrity and trust between the Government and the people of Indonesia.
